Welcome to Delta Upsilon

The University of Minnesota Chapter

Tradition

Established as the seventh oldest extant, all-male, college Greek organization in North America, Delta Upsilon has held its tradition strong since 1834.

Non-Secrecy

Founded as an organization with no intention to hide our rituals, meetings, or pledge process, we focus on what matters most. No frills, just principle.

Intellect

Thousands of alumni and university students all working together towards a common goal of success. In 2013, Business Insider named Delta Upsilon one of the "17 Fraternities with Top Wall Street Alumni."
